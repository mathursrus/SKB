Call Flow Steps

Step 1 — Greeting + Status
"Hello, and thank you for calling Shri Krishna Bhavan! There are currently 5 parties ahead of you, with an estimated wait of about 48 minutes."
Webhook: POST /r/skb/api/voice/incoming → calls getQueueState("skb")
↓
Step 2 — Menu
"To add your name to the waitlist, press 1. To hear the wait time again, press 2. Or hang up at any time."
TwiML: <Gather input="dtmf" numDigits="1" timeout="10">
↓
Press 1 → Join
Step 3a — Name Prompt
"Please say your name after the beep."
TwiML: <Gather input="speech" timeout="5">
Press 2 → Repeat
Step 3b — Repeat Status
(Loops back to Step 1 with fresh queue data)
Webhook: re-calls getQueueState()
Timeout → Goodbye
Step 3c — Goodbye
"Thank you for calling Shri Krishna Bhavan. Goodbye!"
TwiML: <Say> + <Hangup>
↓ (from Press 1)
Step 4 — Party Size
"How many guests in your party? Press a number on your keypad from 1 to 9."
TwiML: <Gather input="dtmf" numDigits="1"> — Caller ID captured from req.body.From
↓
Step 5 — Join Queue + Confirm
"You're all set! You are number 6 in line. Your estimated wait is about 48 minutes. Your pickup code is S-K-B dash 7-Q-3. We'll send you a text message with your code and a link to track your place in line. Thank you for calling!"
Calls joinQueue("skb", { name, partySize, phone }) → sends SMS via joinConfirmationMessage()

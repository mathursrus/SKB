Operator interpretation

This mock keeps the dashboard practical. It should not just count; it should help the owner decide what to inspect next.

What stands out today
The largest measurable leak is before or during phone confirmation. That suggests caller trust and keypad friction matter more than top-of-funnel awareness.
What not to do in v1
Do not add call recording, speech transcript analytics, or a separate vendor analytics stack. The mock assumes structured event persistence only.
Rollout note
Historical funnel data starts when caller-session persistence is shipped. The UI should say that plainly rather than pretending old voice traffic was measured.
